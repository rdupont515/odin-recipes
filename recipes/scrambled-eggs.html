Scrambled Eggs

Description

These scrambled eggs are my preferred way to make breakfast. It is a solid base for a nutritious meal that can be further built upon according to your needs and tastes. I often like to add a side of bacon, or toast with jelly/jam.

Please make any changes to this recipe that you like. Make it yours!

Ingredients

  • 4 of your favorite eggs( I prefer Eggland's Best or Kirkland)
  • 1 tbs of butter, oil, or other cooking fat
  • 1 handful of spinach
  • 1 half of a bell pepper, sliced or chopped
  • chicken stock (I prefer Brookshire's or Kirkland)

Steps

  1. Warm a skillet on Medium heat. Once warm, add your cooking fat of choice and the bell pepper
  2. Cook the peppers over medium heat until they begin to soften, stiring frequently. Then add the spinach, chicken stock, and half of any seasonings you like.
  3. Once the spinach has cooked down most of the way, crack all of the eggs into the skillet. Begin to break the egg yolks and stir everything together in the skillet. You may also add additional seasonings at this time.
  4. Continue stirring the eggs frequently and chopping any large pieces up so that it cooks evenly
  5. Once the eggs appear moist but not runny they are done. Serve with your favorite hot sauce on top!
