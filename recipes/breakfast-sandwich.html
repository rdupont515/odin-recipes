Breakfast Sandwich

Description

I love eating eggs; sometimes I like to make a sandwich if im craving a little more carbs and less veggies. As always, I recommend you make your own changes if your tastes are different from my own.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of sourdough bread
  • 3 Slices of bacon
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Slice of cheddar cheese or 1/4th cup of shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Warm a skillet on Medium heat. Once warm, add the bacon and begin cooking. Do not flip the bacon until one side begins to brown. Then continue to flip as the bacon continues to cook evenly.
  2. Once the bacon is cooked to your satisfaction, place bacon on paper towels. Fry the two eggs in the bacon grease following the same guidelines as cooking the bacon.
  3. Toast the bread so that the outside is slightly browned.
  4. When the toast is finished place the cheese on one slice. Then place the bacon on top of the cheese. You may need to fold or cut the bacon in half.
  5. When the eggs are finished place them on top of the bacon and season to taste. I like to use black pepper hot sauce.
  6. Eat Up!
