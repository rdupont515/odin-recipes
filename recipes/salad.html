Salad

Description

I love having a nice fresh salad for lunch. I'm a big proponent of eating a lot of protein so I usually have a hefty amount of meat on my salads.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of 50/50 spinach and spring mix
  • 5 Baby carrots
  • 1 Half of a bell pepper
  • 5 Grape tomatoes, sliced in half
  • 1 Can of Kirkland Chicken Breast or 2 Cans of Starkist tuna
  • 1/2 Cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 Tbs of your favorite dressing

Steps

  1. Chop the spinach and spring mix.
  2. Slice the baby carrots length wise twice and the in half.
  3. Slice the bell pepper into long pieces and the cut each into three pieces.
  4. Place all ingredients into a bowl and mix.
  5. Enjoy!
